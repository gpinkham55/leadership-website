About Nicole

Igniting Leadership Identity That Lasts

With over 25 years in education, leadership development, and organizational consulting, Nicole Pinkham brings a rare blend of academic rigor and real-world insight to every engagement. After retiring from Auburn University Montgomery in 2018, Nicole founded Impact Training and Consulting to pursue her passion: helping leaders discover who they truly are — and lead from that place.

Nicole's approach is built on the belief that the most effective leaders are authentic and intentional ones. She works alongside executives, emerging leaders, and entire teams to build the self-awareness, emotional intelligence, and strategic clarity needed to lead with lasting impact.
